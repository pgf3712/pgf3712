## About me

I build data and AI products that turn complex information into reliable, understandable systems. I am currently a **Senior Data Scientist at NielsenIQ**, with a multidisciplinary background spanning machine learning, geospatial intelligence, computer vision, cloud data pipelines and creative technology.

My work is especially focused on:

- designing measurable, explainable ML and AI systems;
- working with spatial, multispectral and time-series data;
- building agent architectures with explicit controls, evaluation and observability;
- translating complex technical ideas into clear products and learning experiences.

I am also a PhD candidate researching AI for heritage digitisation and 3D reconstruction. My background in design and visual arts shapes the way I communicate data: technical depth matters, but so do clarity, usability and craft.
